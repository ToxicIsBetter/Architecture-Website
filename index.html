About

Professional Profile

I am an Architect and Interior Designer with 2+ years of UK-aligned studio experience delivering BIM-coordinated projects across commercial, residential, and hospitality sectors. Currently, I am completing my MA in Interior Design at the University of East London, where I received the Best Overall Performance Award 2026 and was a Finalist in the Broadgate Design Competition.

I combine technical precision—construction documentation to UK Building Regulations standards, BIM coordination, and end-to-end project delivery—with a sharp eye for spatial narrative and client growth. My work has expanded studio revenue by 35%, grown active client portfolios by 40%, and reduced project discrepancies by 25%.

I hold a B.Arch with Distinction (SGPA 9.1/10, 3rd in cohort) and a nationally Top-10 nominated thesis. I am based in London with full UK right-to-work and am available immediately.
